The Million Dollar Talk (#TMDT) project was born from a simple challenge between two friends in France, Edouard and John.
They decided to let people express themselves about their sexual experience and thinkings about someone they know or have met.
it is anonymous, quick and completely free.

Sharing a personal sexual experience or a sexual thinking about someone is called The Million Dollar Talk.

You can share your #TMDT about a colleague, a person you met in a train, your girlfriend, your last flirt, everything that has awaken a sexual desire in you.
