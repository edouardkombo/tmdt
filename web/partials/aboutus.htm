#TheMillionDollarTalk project was born from a simple challenge between two friends, Edouard and John, who decided to let people around the world share their sexual experience, winning or frustrating, in real time and anonymously.

Sharing a personal sexual experience online is called a Million Dollar Talk, and is absolutely free.

It is strictly forbidden to harm the dignity of others, this is why messages are not saved and have a 60 seconds lifetime.
